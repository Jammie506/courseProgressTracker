Get Started

This progress tracker is designed to help you stay organized and motivated as you work through your course materials. Track your progress, monitor completed tasks, and see your achievements grow!

How It Works

  • Courses: View all your available courses
  • Modules: Each course contains modules - break down your work into manageable chunks
  • Tasks: Within each module are individual tasks. Complete them one by one
  • Progress Tracking: Watch your progress bar fill as you complete tasks and modules

Tips for Success

  • ✓ Check off tasks as you complete them
  • ✓ Work through modules sequentially for best results
  • ✓ Use the progress bar to stay motivated
  • ✓ Regular practice leads to better outcomes

Need Help?

If you have questions about the course content, refer to the course materials provided by your instructor. This app is designed to help you track progress, not to replace the course content itself.
